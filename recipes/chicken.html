Impress your new Father-In Law Chicken

Mediterranian baked chicken for that special person

This is a midweek chicken dinner idea that’s one of those “throw it all in a pan” recipes with high returns for minimal effort. One of our all-time favourites, to impress that new father-in law of yours.

You are going to need:

  • 1 nordic baking sheet
  • 5 chicken breast

How to make it:

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 200 °C for 5 minutes.
  2. Put the chicken breasts on the baking sheet with no oil or seasoning at all.
  3. Put the baking sheet in the oven and let it cook for about 6 minutes.
IMPORTANT: don't let it cook for more than those 6 minutes, otherwise it's going to get burnt.
