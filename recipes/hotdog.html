The Real College Dudedog

The classic college freshman breakfast

In college it's all about healthy meals that are quick and simple to prepare..

You are going to need:

  • 4 tablespoons strawberry jam
  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1 banana

How to make it:

  1. Cut the bun with a knife, while trying not to cut yourself.
  2. Put the jam inside the bun and spread it with a knife.
  3. Peel a banana a put it on top of the jam.
IMPORTANT: be careful with that butter knife.
